Branding

Brand Name: Memory Restored.

Intended Theme: Utilitarian, Neobrutalist

Brand Questions

What does the company do, or offer?

Sell RAM and NVMe SSDs at pre-inflation (or heavily discounted) prices but in limited quantities and/or limited drops

Who is the company to potential customers? (as a person)

Electronics company selling limited stock of RAM and NVMe SSDs

Why might this company entice someone to purchase a product or service?

Being able to purchase these products at pre-inflation prices saves hundreds of dollars at the tradeoff of being able to purchase them due to limited quantities and drops

Design Attributes

Box Shadows

Elements will have different coloured box shadows depending on function.

Example 1: Content Box Shadow (Green Shadow)

DDR5 RAM NVMe SSD Storage View All Products

Example 2: Button Box Shadow (Black Shadow)

Link Interactions

Links will use the Gold colour in hover and active states, representing the gold contacts in RAM modules that connect directly into the motherboard for modules to be in an active state.

Example: Products Link

Products

Button Interactions

Buttons will contain the box shadows, as well as a tactile-style feedback when hovered/clicked. Buttons will also use Gold as its background colour for high contrast.

Example: Add to Cart Button
